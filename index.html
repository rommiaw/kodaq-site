Privacy Policy & Support

Last updated: May 17, 2026

KODAQ is designed to collect as little as possible — which is nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. KODAQ has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. We do not operate any servers that receive your data, and the app makes no network connections to us.

Your photos

All photos and film rolls are stored only on your device, encrypted at rest. They are never uploaded to us or to any third party.

Camera & Photos permissions

The camera permission is used solely to capture photos into your film roll. The “Add to Photos” permission is used only when you explicitly choose to save photos to your device’s photo library. Neither permission results in any data being sent to us.

Sharing

If you choose to share photos, that happens entirely through Apple’s standard share sheet, directed by you, to recipients you choose. KODAQ is not involved in the transmission and retains nothing. For activities where a text caption makes sense, the share includes a short “Captured with KODAQ” line and a link to the App Store — this is a fixed message with no identifiers or tracking, and it is omitted when you simply save photos to your library.

Children

KODAQ does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
